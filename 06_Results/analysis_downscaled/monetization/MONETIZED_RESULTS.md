## Caveats

- VSL drives ~99% of the total; central Mexico VSL $2.26M (conservative vs US transfer).
- Concentrations are in µg/m³ for all pollutants (NO₂, O₃, GBD O₃, PM₂.₅).
- CVD ED unit cost = admitted hospitalization applied to ED-visit endpoint (likely high).
- Full unit-value derivation + citations: LIT_REVIEW_METHODOLOGY.md. Regenerate via code/30–31.
